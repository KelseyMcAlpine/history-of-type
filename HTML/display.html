History of  Typeface Design

❬               Display               ❭

(1900–present)


Display typefaces, also known as ornamental typefaces are used exclusively for decorative purposes. They have the most distinctive designs of all fonts. There is a wide range of typefaces in this category, each with unique characteristics. Display typefaces are able to set the mood and personality for the rest of a design. They are able to quickly capture the attention of viewers. For this reason, display typefaces are most effective when they are used at large sizes such as headlines and titles. The point size used is typically 30 points or larger. These typefaces are not suitable for body text.
